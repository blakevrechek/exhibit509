8.2 Hosting & cache

Exhibit is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. As the hosting and content-delivery network, Cloudflare receives standard web request data (IP address, user-agent, request URL, timestamp) for every page load, this is what lets the Site serve and lets Cloudflare detect abuse, mitigate denial-of-service attacks, and route requests to the nearest edge location. Cloudflare's data handling is governed by its Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy. Cloudflare may set its own technical cookies under its own privacy practices to operate the security and performance layer; we do not control those and we do not access their contents.

Static files (HTML, CSS, JS, images, the compressed ABA dataset) are cached on Cloudflare's global edge network so the Site loads quickly. Your browser also caches static assets to avoid re-downloading them on repeat visits. Cache headers are public and conservative, HTML refreshes every 5 minutes; images and the dataset bundle cache for one year with an immutable hint (a content change ships under a new filename, so the cache is safe).

The Site also installs a Service Worker (sw.js) that caches the app shell locally on your device for offline use and fast repeat loads. The cache is named after the current build (e.g. exhibit-v1.88.0); when a new build deploys, the old cache is purged automatically on your next visit. You can manually clear it via your browser's site-data settings.

8.3 Third-party assets

To render the map and typography, the Site loads a small number of assets from third parties. When your browser fetches these, those providers may receive your IP address and user-agent and may log them under their own privacy policies. We do not control that processing and we set no cookies through it. The third parties currently used:

  • Fonts, Nunito and Geist Mono are self-hosted from this domain (no request to Google Fonts or any third-party font CDN).

9. Security

The Site is served over HTTPS only. HTTP-to-HTTPS redirection is forced at the edge. HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) is enabled with a one-year max-age + includeSubDomains + the preload directive, so browsers will not allow plaintext connections to this domain. Additional security response headers in effect on every request:

  • X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff, prevents MIME-type confusion attacks.
  • Referrer-Policy: strict-origin-when-cross-origin, limits referrer leakage to third parties.
  • Permissions-Policy, disables geolocation, microphone, camera, and payment APIs that the Site does not use.

If you discover a security issue, please email blake@exhibit509.com rather than disclosing it publicly. We'll respond promptly.
