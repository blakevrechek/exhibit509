## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Northern Kentucky University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 154 (25th–75th percentile 152–157) and a median uGPA of 3.41 (3.15–3.68).

**What is Northern Kentucky University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 73.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Northern Kentucky University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $24,388 per year, and the median grant is $18,000; 41.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Northern Kentucky University?**

84.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
