Common questions about Gonzaga University

What LSAT and GPA do I need for Gonzaga University?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 153–158) and a median uGPA of 3.55 (3.24–3.76).
What is Gonzaga University's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 79.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.3% state average on the same exam.
How much does Gonzaga University cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $57,962 per year, and the median grant is $32,000; 2.1% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at Gonzaga University?
81.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
