Rutgers University School of Law

New Jersey · Public · School ID: rutgers-university

Rutgers University School of Law is a public law school in New Jersey. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 72.8% first-time bar passage rate (state average 75.1%). 92.9% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 12.2% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $31,577 per year; a median grant of $16,000 brings median net tuition to about $15,577. The median LSAT is 158 with a 33.5% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Rutgers University School of Law?

Rutgers University School of Law reported a 72.8% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 75.1% state average.

How much does Rutgers University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at Rutgers University School of Law is $31,577 per year (about $94,731 over three years). The median grant is $16,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $15,577 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Rutgers University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Rutgers University School of Law is 158 and the median GPA is 3.66; the acceptance rate is 33.5%.

What are the job outcomes at Rutgers University School of Law?

92.9% of Rutgers University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 12.2% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 2.4% were still seeking employment.
