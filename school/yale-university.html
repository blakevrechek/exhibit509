Common questions about Yale University

What LSAT and GPA do I need for Yale University?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 174 (25th–75th percentile 171–177) and a median uGPA of 3.96 (3.90–4.00).
What is Yale University's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 96.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.6% state average on the same exam.
How much does Yale University cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $76,636 per year, and the median grant is $34,747; 36.9% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at Yale University?
94.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
