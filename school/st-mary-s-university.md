## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for St. Mary's University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 153 (25th–75th percentile 151–156) and a median uGPA of 3.49 (3.14–3.69).

**What is St. Mary's University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 65.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does St. Mary's University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $44,910 per year, and the median grant is $12,500; 18.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at St. Mary's University?**

86.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
