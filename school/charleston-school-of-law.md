## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Charleston School of Law?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 154 (25th–75th percentile 152–157) and a median uGPA of 3.51 (3.26–3.75).

**What is Charleston School of Law's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 74.1% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 76.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Charleston School of Law cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $48,600 per year, and the median grant is $20,000; 5.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Charleston School of Law?**

81.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
