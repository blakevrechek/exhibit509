## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for California Western School of Law?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 152–158) and a median uGPA of 3.50 (3.16–3.66).

**What is California Western School of Law's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 62.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 77.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does California Western School of Law cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $64,720 per year, and the median grant is $37,440; 39.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at California Western School of Law?**

79.0% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
