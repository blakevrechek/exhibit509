## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Howard University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 156 (25th–75th percentile 153–160) and a median uGPA of 3.63 (3.40–3.80).

**What is Howard University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 76.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 82.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Howard University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $39,780 per year, and the median grant is $23,657; 51.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Howard University?**

70.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
