Jacksonville University School of Law

Florida · Private · School ID: jacksonville-university

Jacksonville University School of Law is a private law school in Florida. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 92.3% first-time bar passage rate (state average 76.5%). 92.3% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 7.7% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $34,800 per year; a median grant of $18,000 brings median net tuition to about $16,800. The median LSAT is 156 with a 35.1% acceptance rate.

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Jacksonville University School of Law?
