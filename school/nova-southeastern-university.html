Common questions about Nova Southeastern University

What LSAT and GPA do I need for Nova Southeastern University?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 152–158) and a median uGPA of 3.52 (3.34–3.71).
What is Nova Southeastern University's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 67.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 72.8% state average on the same exam.
How much does Nova Southeastern University cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $49,350 per year, and the median grant is $15,000; 21.9% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at Nova Southeastern University?
77.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
