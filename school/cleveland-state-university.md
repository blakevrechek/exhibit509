## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Cleveland State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 152–158) and a median uGPA of 3.50 (3.11–3.76).

**What is Cleveland State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 75.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Cleveland State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $29,766 per year, and the median grant is $11,000; 2.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Cleveland State University?**

80.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
