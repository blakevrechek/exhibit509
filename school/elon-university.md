## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Elon University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 154 (25th–75th percentile 151–158) and a median uGPA of 3.40 (3.11–3.70).

**What is Elon University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 76.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 78.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Elon University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $45,333 per year, and the median grant is $23,571; 10.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Elon University?**

88.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
