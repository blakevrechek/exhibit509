## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Northern Illinois University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 150 (25th–75th percentile 148–154) and a median uGPA of 3.41 (3.17–3.64).

**What is Northern Illinois University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 57.1% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 78.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Northern Illinois University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $23,667 per year, and the median grant is $8,274; 22.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Northern Illinois University?**

77.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
