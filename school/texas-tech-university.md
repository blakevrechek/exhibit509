## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Texas Tech University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 159 (25th–75th percentile 157–160) and a median uGPA of 3.69 (3.41–3.85).

**What is Texas Tech University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 86.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Texas Tech University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $22,590 per year, and the median grant is $12,000; 12.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Texas Tech University?**

92.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
