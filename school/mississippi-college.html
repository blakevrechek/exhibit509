Mississippi College School of Law

Mississippi · Private · School ID: mississippi-college

Mississippi College School of Law is a private law school in Mississippi. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 63.4% first-time bar passage rate (state average 79.7%). 92.2% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 1.0% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $37,980 per year; a median grant of $22,000 brings median net tuition to about $15,980. The median LSAT is 152 with a 59.8% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Mississippi College School of Law?

Mississippi College School of Law reported a 63.4% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 79.7% state average.

How much does Mississippi College School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at Mississippi College School of Law is $37,980 per year (about $113,940 over three years). The median grant is $22,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $15,980 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Mississippi College School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Mississippi College School of Law is 152 and the median GPA is 3.37; the acceptance rate is 59.8%.

What are the job outcomes at Mississippi College School of Law?

92.2% of Mississippi College School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 1.0% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 5.8% were still seeking employment.
