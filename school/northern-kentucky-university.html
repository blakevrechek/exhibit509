Northern Kentucky University School of Law

Kentucky · Public · School ID: northern-kentucky-university

Northern Kentucky University School of Law is a public law school in Kentucky. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 73.8% first-time bar passage rate (state average 79.5%). 84.4% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 11.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $24,388 per year; a median grant of $18,000 brings median net tuition to about $6,388. The median LSAT is 154 with a 58.7% acceptance rate.
