## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Arizona State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 165 (25th–75th percentile 158–167) and a median uGPA of 3.91 (3.58–3.98).

**What is Arizona State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 87.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 75.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Arizona State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $28,390 per year, and the median grant is $20,000; 3.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Arizona State University?**

92.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
