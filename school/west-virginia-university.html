West Virginia University School of Law

West Virginia · Public · School ID: west-virginia-university

West Virginia University School of Law is a public law school in West Virginia. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 78.3% first-time bar passage rate (state average 76.5%). 80.2% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 8.8% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $26,370 per year; a median grant of $17,500 brings median net tuition to about $8,870. The median LSAT is 155 with a 40.3% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at West Virginia University School of Law?

West Virginia University School of Law reported a 78.3% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 76.5% state average.

How much does West Virginia University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at West Virginia University School of Law is $26,370 per year (about $79,110 over three years). The median grant is $17,500, bringing median net tuition to roughly $8,870 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for West Virginia University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at West Virginia University School of Law is 155 and the median GPA is 3.65; the acceptance rate is 40.3%.

What are the job outcomes at West Virginia University School of Law?

80.2% of West Virginia University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 8.8% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 9.9% were still seeking employment.
