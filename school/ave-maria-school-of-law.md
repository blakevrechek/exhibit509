## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Ave Maria School of Law?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 152–158) and a median uGPA of 3.36 (3.07–3.66).

**What is Ave Maria School of Law's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 65.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 73.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Ave Maria School of Law cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $49,500 per year, and the median grant is $30,000; 26.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Ave Maria School of Law?**

84.0% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
