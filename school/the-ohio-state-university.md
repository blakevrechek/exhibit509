## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for The Ohio State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 168 (25th–75th percentile 163–169) and a median uGPA of 3.91 (3.64–3.97).

**What is The Ohio State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 92.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does The Ohio State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $35,650 per year, and the median grant is $29,984; 10.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at The Ohio State University?**

93.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
