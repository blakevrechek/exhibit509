## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Chapman University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 163 (25th–75th percentile 156–164) and a median uGPA of 3.70 (3.32–3.82).

**What is Chapman University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 74.1% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 77.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Chapman University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $63,126 per year, and the median grant is $59,500; 41.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Chapman University?**

85.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
