## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Wayne State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 164 (25th–75th percentile 157–166) and a median uGPA of 3.89 (3.56–3.95).

**What is Wayne State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 86.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 72.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Wayne State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $38,861 per year, and the median grant is $34,636; 17.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Wayne State University?**

96.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
