## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Vermont Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 152 (25th–75th percentile 148–157) and a median uGPA of 3.25 (2.81–3.61).

**What is Vermont Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 64.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 76.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Vermont Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $55,382 per year, and the median grant is $28,000; 1.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Vermont Law School?**

77.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
