## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Georgetown University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 171 (25th–75th percentile 166–173) and a median uGPA of 3.93 (3.75–3.98).

**What is Georgetown University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 93.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Georgetown University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $83,576 per year, and the median grant is $35,000; 39.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Georgetown University?**

95.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
