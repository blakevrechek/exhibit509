## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Vanderbilt University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 170 (25th–75th percentile 167–171) and a median uGPA of 3.91 (3.77–3.97).

**What is Vanderbilt University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 94.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Vanderbilt University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $76,440 per year, and the median grant is $32,792; 10.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Vanderbilt University?**

98.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
