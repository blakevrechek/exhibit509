## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Southern Illinois University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 149 (25th–75th percentile 146–155) and a median uGPA of 3.38 (2.93–3.78).

**What is Southern Illinois University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 59.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Southern Illinois University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $15,863 per year, and the median grant is $9,500; 36.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Southern Illinois University?**

81.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
