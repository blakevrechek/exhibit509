## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Seton Hall University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 161 (25th–75th percentile 158–164) and a median uGPA of 3.71 (3.51–3.86).

**What is Seton Hall University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 87.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 75.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Seton Hall University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $69,660 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 11.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Seton Hall University?**

95.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
