## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Florida State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 166 (25th–75th percentile 158–167) and a median uGPA of 3.93 (3.60–3.99).

**What is Florida State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 87.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 73.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Florida State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $19,952 per year, and the median grant is $15,464; 22.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Florida State University?**

92.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
