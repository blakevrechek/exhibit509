## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Campbell University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 156 (25th–75th percentile 152–159) and a median uGPA of 3.51 (3.21–3.77).

**What is Campbell University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 85.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Campbell University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $51,840 per year, and the median grant is $21,771; 1.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Campbell University?**

90.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
