## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Texas A&M University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 169 (25th–75th percentile 161–170) and a median uGPA of 4.00 (3.67–4.00).

**What is Texas A&M University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 96.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Texas A&M University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $32,634 per year, and the median grant is $26,048; 4.8% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Texas A&M University?**

99.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
