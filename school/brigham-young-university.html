Common questions about Brigham Young University

What LSAT and GPA do I need for Brigham Young University?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 170 (25th–75th percentile 168–172) and a median uGPA of 3.95 (3.82–3.98).
What is Brigham Young University's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 90.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.6% state average on the same exam.
How much does Brigham Young University cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $15,992 per year, and the median grant is $11,646; 1.7% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at Brigham Young University?
94.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
