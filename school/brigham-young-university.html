Brigham Young University School of Law

Utah · Private · School ID: brigham-young-university

Brigham Young University School of Law is a private law school in Utah. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 90.4% first-time bar passage rate (state average 83.6%). 94.5% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 29.1% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $15,992 per year; a median grant of $11,646 brings median net tuition to about $4,346. The median LSAT is 170 with a 22.8% acceptance rate.
