## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Seattle University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 159 (25th–75th percentile 155–162) and a median uGPA of 3.60 (3.22–3.80).

**What is Seattle University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 78.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 82.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Seattle University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $59,769 per year, and the median grant is $30,000; 12.8% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Seattle University?**

82.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
