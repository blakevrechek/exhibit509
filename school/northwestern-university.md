## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Northwestern University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 173 (25th–75th percentile 167–175) and a median uGPA of 3.96 (3.76–4.00).

**What is Northwestern University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 91.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Northwestern University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $79,772 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 23.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Northwestern University?**

97.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
