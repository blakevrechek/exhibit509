## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Villanova University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 164 (25th–75th percentile 159–166) and a median uGPA of 3.80 (3.64–3.90).

**What is Villanova University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 91.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.1% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Villanova University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $59,800 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 13.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Villanova University?**

96.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
