## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Oklahoma City University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 151 (25th–75th percentile 149–155) and a median uGPA of 3.56 (3.21–3.78).

**What is Oklahoma City University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 75.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Oklahoma City University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $32,103 per year, and the median grant is $14,000; 22.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Oklahoma City University?**

82.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
