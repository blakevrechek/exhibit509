## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Washington and Lee University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 167 (25th–75th percentile 161–168) and a median uGPA of 3.75 (3.51–3.85).

**What is Washington and Lee University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 89.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.1% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Washington and Lee University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $57,450 per year, and the median grant is $33,500; 3.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Washington and Lee University?**

92.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
