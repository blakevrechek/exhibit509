## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for St. John's University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 164 (25th–75th percentile 156–166) and a median uGPA of 3.81 (3.44–3.91).

**What is St. John's University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 87.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 86.1% state average on the same exam.

**How much does St. John's University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $75,170 per year, and the median grant is $60,000; 15.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at St. John's University?**

92.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
