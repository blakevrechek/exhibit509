University of Pennsylvania Carey Law School

Pennsylvania · Private · School ID: pennsylvania-university-of

University of Pennsylvania Carey Law School is a private law school in Pennsylvania. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 96.7% first-time bar passage rate (state average 82.9%). 99.6% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 70.7% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $78,348 per year; a median grant of $42,246 brings median net tuition to about $36,102. The median LSAT is 173 with a 8.1% acceptance rate.
