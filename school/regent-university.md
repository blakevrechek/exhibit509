## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Regent University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 158 (25th–75th percentile 153–161) and a median uGPA of 3.68 (3.37–3.89).

**What is Regent University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 88.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 77.6% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Regent University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $40,600 per year, and the median grant is $22,500; 3.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Regent University?**

96.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
