## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Atlanta's John Marshall Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 152 (25th–75th percentile 150–155) and a median uGPA of 3.13 (2.78–3.49).

**What is Atlanta's John Marshall Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 68.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Atlanta's John Marshall Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $52,536 per year, and the median grant is $4,500; 15.8% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Atlanta's John Marshall Law School?**

83.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
