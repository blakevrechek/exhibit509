## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Boston University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 170 (25th–75th percentile 164–171) and a median uGPA of 3.88 (3.71–3.93).

**What is Boston University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 92.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Boston University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $69,870 per year, and the median grant is $29,000; 8.8% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Boston University?**

96.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
