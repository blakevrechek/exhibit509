## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for William & Mary?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 166 (25th–75th percentile 161–167) and a median uGPA of 3.82 (3.53–3.94).

**What is William & Mary's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 83.6% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does William & Mary cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $38,734 per year, and the median grant is $29,302; 34.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at William & Mary?**

93.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
