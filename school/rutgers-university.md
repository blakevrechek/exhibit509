## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Rutgers University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 158 (25th–75th percentile 155–161) and a median uGPA of 3.66 (3.35–3.82).

**What is Rutgers University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 72.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 75.1% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Rutgers University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $31,577 per year, and the median grant is $16,000; 13.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Rutgers University?**

92.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
