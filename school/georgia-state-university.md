## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Georgia State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 160 (25th–75th percentile 158–163) and a median uGPA of 3.64 (3.45–3.82).

**What is Georgia State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 90.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Georgia State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $16,162 per year, and the median grant is $7,776; 51.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Georgia State University?**

92.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
