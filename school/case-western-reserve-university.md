## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Case Western Reserve University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 162 (25th–75th percentile 156–164) and a median uGPA of 3.76 (3.51–3.89).

**What is Case Western Reserve University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 82.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Case Western Reserve University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $64,600 per year, and the median grant is $42,500; 2.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Case Western Reserve University?**

81.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
