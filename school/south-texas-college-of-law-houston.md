## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for South Texas College of Law Houston?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 153–158) and a median uGPA of 3.41 (3.08–3.65).

**What is South Texas College of Law Houston's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 91.1% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does South Texas College of Law Houston cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $42,960 per year, and the median grant is $11,000; 15.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at South Texas College of Law Houston?**

87.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
