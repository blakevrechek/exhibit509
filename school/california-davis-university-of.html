University of California-Davis School of Law

California · Public · School ID: california-davis-university-of

University of California-Davis School of Law is a public law school in California. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 88.9% first-time bar passage rate (state average 77.8%). 94.6% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 25.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $57,460 per year; a median grant of $25,000 brings median net tuition to about $32,460. The median LSAT is 165 with a 21.1% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of California-Davis School of Law?

University of California-Davis School of Law reported a 88.9% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 77.8% state average.

How much does University of California-Davis School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of California-Davis School of Law is $57,460 per year (about $172,380 over three years). The median grant is $25,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $32,460 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of California-Davis School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of California-Davis School of Law is 165 and the median GPA is 3.70; the acceptance rate is 21.1%.

What are the job outcomes at University of California-Davis School of Law?

94.6% of University of California-Davis School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 25.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 0.5% were still seeking employment.
