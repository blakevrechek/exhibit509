## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Widener University Commonwealth Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 150 (25th–75th percentile 147–153) and a median uGPA of 3.33 (2.98–3.64).

**What is Widener University Commonwealth Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 54.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.1% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Widener University Commonwealth Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $54,375 per year, and the median grant is $25,000; 5.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Widener University Commonwealth Law School?**

84.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
