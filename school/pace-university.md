## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Pace University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 154 (25th–75th percentile 151–157) and a median uGPA of 3.54 (3.31–3.74).

**What is Pace University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 68.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 84.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Pace University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $56,648 per year, and the median grant is $29,000; 6.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Pace University?**

88.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
