University of St. Thomas (Minnesota) School of Law

Minnesota · Private · School ID: st-thomas-(minneapolis)-university-of

University of St. Thomas (Minnesota) School of Law is a private law school in Minnesota. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 74.7% first-time bar passage rate (state average 84.4%). 91.9% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 6.0% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $50,998 per year; a median grant of $26,865 brings median net tuition to about $24,133. The median LSAT is 159 with a 57.6% acceptance rate.

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of St. Thomas (Minnesota) School of Law?

How much does University of St. Thomas (Minnesota) School of Law cost?
