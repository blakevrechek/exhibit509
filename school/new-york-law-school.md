## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for New York Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 157 (25th–75th percentile 154–160) and a median uGPA of 3.63 (3.41–3.78).

**What is New York Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 81.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 84.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does New York Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $71,052 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 14.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at New York Law School?**

85.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
