## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Stetson University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 159 (25th–75th percentile 157–162) and a median uGPA of 3.63 (3.42–3.81).

**What is Stetson University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 77.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 73.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Stetson University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $56,521 per year, and the median grant is $25,000; 11.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Stetson University?**

87.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
