## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Cooley Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 147 (25th–75th percentile 145–151) and a median uGPA of 3.09 (2.67–3.45).

**What is Cooley Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 51.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 74.1% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Cooley Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $45,820 per year, and the median grant is $13,746; 43.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Cooley Law School?**

74.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
