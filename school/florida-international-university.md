## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Florida International University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 161 (25th–75th percentile 159–162) and a median uGPA of 3.80 (3.66–3.89).

**What is Florida International University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 88.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 72.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Florida International University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $20,039 per year, and the median grant is $10,365; 44.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Florida International University?**

93.0% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
