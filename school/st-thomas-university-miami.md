## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for St. Thomas University (Miami)?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 152 (25th–75th percentile 150–156) and a median uGPA of 3.42 (3.12–3.65).

**What is St. Thomas University (Miami)'s bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 80.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 72.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does St. Thomas University (Miami) cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $44,730 per year, and the median grant is $25,320; 35.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at St. Thomas University (Miami)?**

79.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
