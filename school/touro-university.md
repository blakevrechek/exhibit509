## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Touro University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 153 (25th–75th percentile 151–156) and a median uGPA of 3.35 (3.05–3.66).

**What is Touro University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 61.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Touro University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $61,000 per year, and the median grant is $22,000; 23.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Touro University?**

84.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
