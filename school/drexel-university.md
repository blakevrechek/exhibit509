## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Drexel University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 160 (25th–75th percentile 154–162) and a median uGPA of 3.76 (3.35–3.87).

**What is Drexel University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 85.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Drexel University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $57,000 per year, and the median grant is $28,667; 24.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Drexel University?**

89.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
