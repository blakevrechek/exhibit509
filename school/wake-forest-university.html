Common questions about Wake Forest University

What LSAT and GPA do I need for Wake Forest University?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 166 (25th–75th percentile 163–168) and a median uGPA of 3.79 (3.60–3.90).
What is Wake Forest University's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 88.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.3% state average on the same exam.
How much does Wake Forest University cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $57,920 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 24.2% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at Wake Forest University?
98.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
