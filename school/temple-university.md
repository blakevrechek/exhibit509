## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Temple University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 165 (25th–75th percentile 162–168) and a median uGPA of 3.76 (3.59–3.90).

**What is Temple University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 90.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Temple University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $32,588 per year, and the median grant is $31,334; 14.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Temple University?**

95.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
