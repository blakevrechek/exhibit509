## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Capital University Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 151 (25th–75th percentile 148–157) and a median uGPA of 3.44 (3.07–3.66).

**What is Capital University Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 72.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Capital University Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $45,066 per year, and the median grant is $21,000; 3.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Capital University Law School?**

84.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
