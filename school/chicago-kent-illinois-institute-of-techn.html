Chicago-Kent, Illinois Institute of Technology School of Law

Illinois · Private · School ID: chicago-kent-illinois-institute-of-techn

Chicago-Kent, Illinois Institute of Technology School of Law is a private law school in Illinois. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 79.0% first-time bar passage rate (state average 78.7%). 84.5% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 14.2% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $53,070 per year; a median grant of $35,000 brings median net tuition to about $18,070. The median LSAT is 161 with a 38.6% acceptance rate.
