## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Mississippi College?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 152 (25th–75th percentile 148–155) and a median uGPA of 3.37 (3.03–3.73).

**What is Mississippi College's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 63.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Mississippi College cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $37,980 per year, and the median grant is $22,000; 35.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Mississippi College?**

92.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
