## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for New England Law/Boston?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 153 (25th–75th percentile 149–157) and a median uGPA of 3.37 (3.06–3.68).

**What is New England Law/Boston's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 79.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does New England Law/Boston cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $62,896 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 11.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at New England Law/Boston?**

67.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
