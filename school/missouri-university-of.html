University of Missouri School of Law

Missouri · Public · School ID: missouri-university-of

University of Missouri School of Law is a public law school in Missouri. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 83.3% first-time bar passage rate (state average 82.1%). 96.0% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 10.4% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $23,548 per year; a median grant of $17,500 brings median net tuition to about $6,048. The median LSAT is 161 with a 42.0% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of Missouri School of Law?

University of Missouri School of Law reported a 83.3% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 82.1% state average.

How much does University of Missouri School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of Missouri School of Law is $23,548 per year (about $70,644 over three years). The median grant is $17,500, bringing median net tuition to roughly $6,048 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of Missouri School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of Missouri School of Law is 161 and the median GPA is 3.72; the acceptance rate is 42.0%.

What are the job outcomes at University of Missouri School of Law?

96.0% of University of Missouri School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 10.4% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 3.2% were still seeking employment.
