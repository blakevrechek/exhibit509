## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for New York University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 172 (25th–75th percentile 169–174) and a median uGPA of 3.92 (3.81–3.97).

**What is New York University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 96.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does New York University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $83,952 per year, and the median grant is $20,000; 34.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at New York University?**

99.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
