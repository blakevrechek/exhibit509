## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Albany Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 158 (25th–75th percentile 154–161) and a median uGPA of 3.54 (3.16–3.75).

**What is Albany Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 74.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Albany Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $63,994 per year, and the median grant is $38,000; 6.0% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Albany Law School?**

91.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
