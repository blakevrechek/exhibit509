## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Southern University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 147 (25th–75th percentile 145–150) and a median uGPA of 3.15 (2.86–3.48).

**What is Southern University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 60.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 75.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Southern University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $11,338 per year, and the median grant is $11,000; 79.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Southern University?**

54.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
