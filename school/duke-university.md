## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Duke University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 171 (25th–75th percentile 169–172) and a median uGPA of 3.91 (3.83–3.96).

**What is Duke University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 97.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 82.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Duke University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $80,100 per year, and the median grant is $35,000; 5.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Duke University?**

98.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
