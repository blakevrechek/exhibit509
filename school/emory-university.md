## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Emory University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 166 (25th–75th percentile 162–168) and a median uGPA of 3.82 (3.68–3.87).

**What is Emory University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 88.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Emory University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $69,510 per year, and the median grant is $36,000; 1.8% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Emory University?**

91.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
