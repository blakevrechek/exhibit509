## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Barry University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 150 (25th–75th percentile 148–152) and a median uGPA of 3.23 (2.93–3.48).

**What is Barry University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 68.6% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 73.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Barry University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $42,000 per year, and the median grant is $16,000; 21.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Barry University?**

72.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
