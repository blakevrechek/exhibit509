## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Tulane University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 161 (25th–75th percentile 159–164) and a median uGPA of 3.67 (3.47–3.81).

**What is Tulane University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 77.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 79.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Tulane University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $69,578 per year, and the median grant is $37,500; 13.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Tulane University?**

89.7% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
