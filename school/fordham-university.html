Fordham University School of Law

New York · Private · School ID: fordham-university

Fordham University School of Law is a private law school in New York. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 93.0% first-time bar passage rate (state average 85.8%). 91.2% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 50.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $78,078 per year; a median grant of $35,000 brings median net tuition to about $43,078. The median LSAT is 168 with a 16.2% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Fordham University School of Law?

Fordham University School of Law reported a 93.0% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 85.8% state average.

How much does Fordham University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at Fordham University School of Law is $78,078 per year (about $234,234 over three years). The median grant is $35,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $43,078 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Fordham University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Fordham University School of Law is 168 and the median GPA is 3.79; the acceptance rate is 16.2%.

What are the job outcomes at Fordham University School of Law?

91.2% of Fordham University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 50.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 4.8% were still seeking employment.
