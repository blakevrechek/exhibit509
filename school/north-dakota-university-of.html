University of North Dakota School of Law

North Dakota · Public · School ID: north-dakota-university-of

University of North Dakota School of Law is a public law school in North Dakota. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 68.3% first-time bar passage rate (state average 73.3%). 83.6% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs. Resident tuition is $17,139 per year; a median grant of $3,875 brings median net tuition to about $13,264. The median LSAT is 151 with a 63.1% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of North Dakota School of Law?

University of North Dakota School of Law reported a 68.3% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 73.3% state average.

How much does University of North Dakota School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of North Dakota School of Law is $17,139 per year (about $51,417 over three years). The median grant is $3,875, bringing median net tuition to roughly $13,264 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of North Dakota School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of North Dakota School of Law is 151 and the median GPA is 3.48; the acceptance rate is 63.1%.

What are the job outcomes at University of North Dakota School of Law?

83.6% of University of North Dakota School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 4.1% were still seeking employment.
