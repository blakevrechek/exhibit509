## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Syracuse University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 158 (25th–75th percentile 154–160) and a median uGPA of 3.60 (3.18–3.77).

**What is Syracuse University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 82.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 82.6% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Syracuse University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $64,650 per year, and the median grant is $24,351; 8.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Syracuse University?**

90.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
