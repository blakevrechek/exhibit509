## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Texas Southern University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 150 (25th–75th percentile 148–152) and a median uGPA of 3.33 (2.99–3.62).

**What is Texas Southern University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 72.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Texas Southern University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $19,202 per year, and the median grant is $7,363; 48.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Texas Southern University?**

57.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
