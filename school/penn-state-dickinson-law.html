Penn State Dickinson Law

Pennsylvania · Public · School ID: penn-state-dickinson-law

Penn State Dickinson Law is a public law school in Pennsylvania. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 87.0% first-time bar passage rate (state average 79.7%). 87.0% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 8.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $60,048 per year; a median grant of $36,000 brings median net tuition to about $24,048. The median LSAT is 159 with a 43.0% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Penn State Dickinson Law?

Penn State Dickinson Law reported a 87.0% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 79.7% state average.

How much does Penn State Dickinson Law cost?

Resident tuition at Penn State Dickinson Law is $60,048 per year (about $180,144 over three years). The median grant is $36,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $24,048 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Penn State Dickinson Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Penn State Dickinson Law is 159 and the median GPA is 3.71; the acceptance rate is 43.0%.

What are the job outcomes at Penn State Dickinson Law?

87.0% of Penn State Dickinson Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 8.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 5.8% were still seeking employment.
