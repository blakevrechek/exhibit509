## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Roger Williams University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 150 (25th–75th percentile 147–154) and a median uGPA of 3.43 (3.02–3.73).

**What is Roger Williams University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 57.1% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 76.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Roger Williams University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $47,400 per year, and the median grant is $28,000; 35.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Roger Williams University?**

82.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
