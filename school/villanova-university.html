Villanova University School of Law

Pennsylvania · Private · School ID: villanova-university

Villanova University School of Law is a private law school in Pennsylvania. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 91.3% first-time bar passage rate (state average 80.1%). 96.2% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 23.4% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $59,800 per year; a median grant of $40,000 brings median net tuition to about $19,800. The median LSAT is 164 with a 25.9% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Villanova University School of Law?

Villanova University School of Law reported a 91.3% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 80.1% state average.

How much does Villanova University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at Villanova University School of Law is $59,800 per year (about $179,400 over three years). The median grant is $40,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $19,800 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Villanova University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Villanova University School of Law is 164 and the median GPA is 3.80; the acceptance rate is 25.9%.

What are the job outcomes at Villanova University School of Law?

96.2% of Villanova University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 23.4% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 1.6% were still seeking employment.
