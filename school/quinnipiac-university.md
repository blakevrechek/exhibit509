## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Quinnipiac University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 153–158) and a median uGPA of 3.65 (3.41–3.84).

**What is Quinnipiac University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 73.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 73.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Quinnipiac University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $56,610 per year, and the median grant is $25,000; 14.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Quinnipiac University?**

88.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
