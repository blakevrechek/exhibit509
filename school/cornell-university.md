## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Cornell University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 173 (25th–75th percentile 168–175) and a median uGPA of 3.92 (3.75–3.97).

**What is Cornell University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 96.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 84.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Cornell University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $84,722 per year, and the median grant is $30,000; 9.3% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Cornell University?**

99.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
