## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Saint Louis University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 157 (25th–75th percentile 154–161) and a median uGPA of 3.61 (3.37–3.83).

**What is Saint Louis University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 84.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Saint Louis University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $55,260 per year, and the median grant is $25,000; 2.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Saint Louis University?**

93.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
