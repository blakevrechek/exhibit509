## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Washington University (St. Louis)?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 175 (25th–75th percentile 165–176) and a median uGPA of 3.96 (3.58–4.00).

**What is Washington University (St. Louis)'s bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 93.6% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Washington University (St. Louis) cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $72,792 per year, and the median grant is $48,000; 5.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Washington University (St. Louis)?**

97.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
