University of Idaho School of Law

Idaho · Public · School ID: idaho-university-of

University of Idaho School of Law is a public law school in Idaho. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 68.4% first-time bar passage rate (state average 78.4%). 85.4% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 1.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $27,516 per year; a median grant of $8,000 brings median net tuition to about $19,516. The median LSAT is 153 with a 66.8% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of Idaho School of Law?

University of Idaho School of Law reported a 68.4% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 78.4% state average.

How much does University of Idaho School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of Idaho School of Law is $27,516 per year (about $82,548 over three years). The median grant is $8,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $19,516 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of Idaho School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of Idaho School of Law is 153 and the median GPA is 3.54; the acceptance rate is 66.8%.

What are the job outcomes at University of Idaho School of Law?

85.4% of University of Idaho School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 1.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 8.8% were still seeking employment.
