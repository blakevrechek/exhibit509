## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Marquette University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 158 (25th–75th percentile 156–160) and a median uGPA of 3.70 (3.50–3.83).

**What is Marquette University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 50.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 70.9% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Marquette University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $51,970 per year, and the median grant is $23,000; 5.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Marquette University?**

95.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
