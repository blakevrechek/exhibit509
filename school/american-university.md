## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for American University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 162 (25th–75th percentile 157–163) and a median uGPA of 3.63 (3.32–3.75).

**What is American University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 81.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 82.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does American University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $66,990 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 40.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at American University?**

82.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
