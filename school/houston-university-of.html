University of Houston School of Law

Texas · Public · School ID: houston-university-of

University of Houston School of Law is a public law school in Texas. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 84.5% first-time bar passage rate (state average 82.5%). 92.4% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 27.3% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $31,326 per year; a median grant of $11,500 brings median net tuition to about $19,826. The median LSAT is 163 with a 23.0% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of Houston School of Law?

University of Houston School of Law reported a 84.5% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 82.5% state average.

How much does University of Houston School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of Houston School of Law is $31,326 per year (about $93,978 over three years). The median grant is $11,500, bringing median net tuition to roughly $19,826 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of Houston School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of Houston School of Law is 163 and the median GPA is 3.79; the acceptance rate is 23.0%.

What are the job outcomes at University of Houston School of Law?

92.4% of University of Houston School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 27.3% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 3.4% were still seeking employment.
