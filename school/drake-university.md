## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Drake University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 156 (25th–75th percentile 153–159) and a median uGPA of 3.67 (3.38–3.89).

**What is Drake University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 85.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Drake University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $50,920 per year, and the median grant is $31,000; 18.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Drake University?**

89.5% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
