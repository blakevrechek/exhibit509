## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Faulkner University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 150 (25th–75th percentile 148–154) and a median uGPA of 3.33 (2.99–3.66).

**What is Faulkner University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 71.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.6% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Faulkner University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $39,900 per year, and the median grant is $15,960; 28.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Faulkner University?**

78.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
