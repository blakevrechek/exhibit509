University of Virginia School of Law

Virginia · Public · School ID: virginia-university-of

University of Virginia School of Law is a public law school in Virginia. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 95.0% first-time bar passage rate (state average 82.8%). 98.7% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 67.6% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $74,078 per year; a median grant of $35,000 brings median net tuition to about $39,078. The median LSAT is 173 with a 10.2% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of Virginia School of Law?

University of Virginia School of Law reported a 95.0% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 82.8% state average.

How much does University of Virginia School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of Virginia School of Law is $74,078 per year (about $222,234 over three years). The median grant is $35,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $39,078 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of Virginia School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of Virginia School of Law is 173 and the median GPA is 3.99; the acceptance rate is 10.2%.

What are the job outcomes at University of Virginia School of Law?

98.7% of University of Virginia School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 67.6% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 1.0% were still seeking employment.
