## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Columbia University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 173 (25th–75th percentile 169–175) and a median uGPA of 3.92 (3.85–3.98).

**What is Columbia University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 95.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Columbia University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $85,368 per year, and the median grant is $32,000; 46.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Columbia University?**

97.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
