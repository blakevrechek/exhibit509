## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Willamette University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 154 (25th–75th percentile 151–157) and a median uGPA of 3.48 (3.22–3.70).

**What is Willamette University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 66.7% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 75.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Willamette University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $56,600 per year, and the median grant is $20,000; 6.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Willamette University?**

76.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
