## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Creighton University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 153 (25th–75th percentile 150–156) and a median uGPA of 3.51 (3.14–3.74).

**What is Creighton University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 72.6% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 82.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Creighton University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $44,718 per year, and the median grant is $25,600; 10.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Creighton University?**

90.3% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
