## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Pepperdine University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 164 (25th–75th percentile 161–167) and a median uGPA of 3.85 (3.61–3.94).

**What is Pepperdine University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 86.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 78.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Pepperdine University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $72,920 per year, and the median grant is $30,000; 22.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Pepperdine University?**

93.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
