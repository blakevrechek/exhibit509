## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Lewis & Clark Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 161 (25th–75th percentile 158–166) and a median uGPA of 3.59 (3.24–3.79).

**What is Lewis & Clark Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 85.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 75.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Lewis & Clark Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $61,434 per year, and the median grant is $26,000; 4.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Lewis & Clark Law School?**

88.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
