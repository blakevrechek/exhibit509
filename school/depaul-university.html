DePaul University School of Law

Illinois · Private · School ID: depaul-university

DePaul University School of Law is a private law school in Illinois. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 76.0% first-time bar passage rate (state average 78.6%). 88.4% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 11.0% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $55,328 per year; a median grant of $34,950 brings median net tuition to about $20,378. The median LSAT is 158 with a 34.8% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at DePaul University School of Law?

DePaul University School of Law reported a 76.0% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 78.6% state average.

How much does DePaul University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at DePaul University School of Law is $55,328 per year (about $165,984 over three years). The median grant is $34,950, bringing median net tuition to roughly $20,378 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for DePaul University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at DePaul University School of Law is 158 and the median GPA is 3.61; the acceptance rate is 34.8%.

What are the job outcomes at DePaul University School of Law?

88.4% of DePaul University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 11.0% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 4.5% were still seeking employment.
