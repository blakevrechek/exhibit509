Common questions about DePaul University

What LSAT and GPA do I need for DePaul University?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 158 (25th–75th percentile 154–160) and a median uGPA of 3.61 (3.17–3.80).
What is DePaul University's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 76.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 78.6% state average on the same exam.
How much does DePaul University cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $55,328 per year, and the median grant is $34,950; 0.8% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at DePaul University?
88.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
