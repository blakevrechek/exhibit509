Inter American University of Puerto Rico School of Law

Puerto Rico · Private · School ID: inter-american-university-of-puerto-rico

Inter American University of Puerto Rico School of Law is a private law school in Puerto Rico. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 48.2% first-time bar passage rate (state average 52.0%). 61.9% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 0.5% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $16,585 per year; a median grant of $2,379 brings median net tuition to about $14,206. The median LSAT is 144 with a 50.9% acceptance rate.
