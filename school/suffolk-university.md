## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Suffolk University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 152–159) and a median uGPA of 3.58 (3.37–3.79).

**What is Suffolk University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 79.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Suffolk University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $62,610 per year, and the median grant is $22,445; 19.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Suffolk University?**

89.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
