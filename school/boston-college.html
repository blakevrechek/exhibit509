Common questions about Boston College

What LSAT and GPA do I need for Boston College?
The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 168 (25th–75th percentile 162–169) and a median uGPA of 3.83 (3.67–3.90).
What is Boston College's bar passage rate?
The first-time bar passage rate was 93.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.5% state average on the same exam.
How much does Boston College cost?
Sticker resident tuition is $72,380 per year, and the median grant is $24,000; 10.9% of students receive no grant.
What are employment outcomes at Boston College?
95.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
