## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Stanford University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 173 (25th–75th percentile 171–176) and a median uGPA of 3.96 (3.87–4.00).

**What is Stanford University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 96.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 81.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Stanford University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $77,454 per year, and the median grant is $52,797; 49.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Stanford University?**

91.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
