Southern Methodist University School of Law

Texas · Private · School ID: southern-methodist-university

Southern Methodist University School of Law is a private law school in Texas. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 90.2% first-time bar passage rate (state average 81.4%). 98.2% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 38.2% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $60,844 per year; a median grant of $26,000 brings median net tuition to about $34,844. The median LSAT is 167 with a 24.6% acceptance rate.
