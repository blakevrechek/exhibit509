## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Hofstra University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 157 (25th–75th percentile 154–160) and a median uGPA of 3.65 (3.35–3.83).

**What is Hofstra University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 78.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Hofstra University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $73,344 per year, and the median grant is $35,793; 16.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Hofstra University?**

90.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
