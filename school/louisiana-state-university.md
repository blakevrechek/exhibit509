## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Louisiana State University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 157 (25th–75th percentile 155–160) and a median uGPA of 3.74 (3.48–3.93).

**What is Louisiana State University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 84.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 74.6% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Louisiana State University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $19,750 per year, and the median grant is $19,750; 21.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Louisiana State University?**

88.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
