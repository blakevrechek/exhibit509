## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Inter American University of Puerto Rico?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 144 (25th–75th percentile 140–148) and a median uGPA of 3.39 (3.18–3.65).

**What is Inter American University of Puerto Rico's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 48.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 52.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Inter American University of Puerto Rico cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $16,585 per year, and the median grant is $2,379; 92.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Inter American University of Puerto Rico?**

61.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
