## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for West Virginia University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 151–159) and a median uGPA of 3.65 (3.34–3.89).

**What is West Virginia University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 78.3% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 76.5% state average on the same exam.

**How much does West Virginia University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $26,370 per year, and the median grant is $17,500; 22.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at West Virginia University?**

80.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
