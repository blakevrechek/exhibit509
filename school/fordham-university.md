## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Fordham University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 168 (25th–75th percentile 165–170) and a median uGPA of 3.79 (3.60–3.88).

**What is Fordham University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 93.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Fordham University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $78,078 per year, and the median grant is $35,000; 13.0% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Fordham University?**

91.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
