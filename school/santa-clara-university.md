## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Santa Clara University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 159 (25th–75th percentile 157–162) and a median uGPA of 3.50 (3.21–3.74).

**What is Santa Clara University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 74.8% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 78.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Santa Clara University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $65,540 per year, and the median grant is $40,000; 38.6% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Santa Clara University?**

79.8% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
