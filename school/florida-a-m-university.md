## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Florida A&M University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 152 (25th–75th percentile 149–155) and a median uGPA of 3.48 (3.18–3.74).

**What is Florida A&M University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 75.4% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 73.3% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Florida A&M University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $13,224 per year, and the median grant is $7,500; 55.4% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Florida A&M University?**

79.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
