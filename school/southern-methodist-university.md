## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Southern Methodist University?**

**What is Southern Methodist University's bar passage rate?**

**How much does Southern Methodist University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $60,844 per year, and the median grant is $26,000; 5.9% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Southern Methodist University?**

98.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
