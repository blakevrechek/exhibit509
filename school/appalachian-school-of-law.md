## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Appalachian School of Law?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 146 (25th–75th percentile 145–151) and a median uGPA of 3.20 (2.72–3.53).

**What is Appalachian School of Law's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 63.2% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 77.2% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Appalachian School of Law cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $41,000 per year, and the median grant is $10,250; 13.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Appalachian School of Law?**

65.1% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
