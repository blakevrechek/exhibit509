## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for George Mason University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 169 (25th–75th percentile 162–171) and a median uGPA of 3.93 (3.55–3.98).

**What is George Mason University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 86.0% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does George Mason University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $24,864 per year, and the median grant is $21,000; 16.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at George Mason University?**

97.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
