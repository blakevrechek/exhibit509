University of Louisville School of Law

Kentucky · Public · School ID: louisville-university-of

University of Louisville School of Law is a public law school in Kentucky. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 75.6% first-time bar passage rate (state average 78.5%). 93.3% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 15.1% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $27,000 per year; a median grant of $12,000 brings median net tuition to about $15,000. The median LSAT is 157 with a 63.4% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at University of Louisville School of Law?

University of Louisville School of Law reported a 75.6% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 78.5% state average.

How much does University of Louisville School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at University of Louisville School of Law is $27,000 per year (about $81,000 over three years). The median grant is $12,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $15,000 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for University of Louisville School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at University of Louisville School of Law is 157 and the median GPA is 3.67; the acceptance rate is 63.4%.

What are the job outcomes at University of Louisville School of Law?

93.3% of University of Louisville School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 15.1% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 3.4% were still seeking employment.
