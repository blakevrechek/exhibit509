## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Brooklyn Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 161 (25th–75th percentile 158–163) and a median uGPA of 3.59 (3.37–3.75).

**What is Brooklyn Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 84.5% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 85.7% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Brooklyn Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $75,496 per year, and the median grant is $34,000; 8.7% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Brooklyn Law School?**

91.6% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
