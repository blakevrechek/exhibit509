## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Harvard University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 174 (25th–75th percentile 171–176) and a median uGPA of 3.96 (3.89–4.00).

**What is Harvard University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 97.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 83.4% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Harvard University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $77,100 per year, and the median grant is $27,510; 62.1% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Harvard University?**

97.2% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
