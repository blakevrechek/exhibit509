Widener University Delaware Law School

Pennsylvania · Private · School ID: widener-university-delaware-law-school

Widener University Delaware Law School is a private law school in Pennsylvania. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 66.3% first-time bar passage rate (state average 76.5%). 90.8% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 3.9% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $56,550 per year; a median grant of $32,000 brings median net tuition to about $24,550. The median LSAT is 152 with a 59.7% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Widener University Delaware Law School?

Widener University Delaware Law School reported a 66.3% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 76.5% state average.

How much does Widener University Delaware Law School cost?

Resident tuition at Widener University Delaware Law School is $56,550 per year (about $169,650 over three years). The median grant is $32,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $24,550 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Widener University Delaware Law School?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Widener University Delaware Law School is 152 and the median GPA is 3.33; the acceptance rate is 59.7%.

What are the job outcomes at Widener University Delaware Law School?

90.8% of Widener University Delaware Law School graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 3.9% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 6.3% were still seeking employment.
