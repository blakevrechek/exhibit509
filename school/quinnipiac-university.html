Quinnipiac University School of Law

Connecticut · Private · School ID: quinnipiac-university

Quinnipiac University School of Law is a private law school in Connecticut. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 73.7% first-time bar passage rate (state average 73.9%). 88.6% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 1.6% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $56,610 per year; a median grant of $25,000 brings median net tuition to about $31,610. The median LSAT is 155 with a 43.1% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Quinnipiac University School of Law?

Quinnipiac University School of Law reported a 73.7% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 73.9% state average.

How much does Quinnipiac University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at Quinnipiac University School of Law is $56,610 per year (about $169,830 over three years). The median grant is $25,000, bringing median net tuition to roughly $31,610 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Quinnipiac University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Quinnipiac University School of Law is 155 and the median GPA is 3.65; the acceptance rate is 43.1%.

What are the job outcomes at Quinnipiac University School of Law?

88.6% of Quinnipiac University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 1.6% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 4.9% were still seeking employment.
