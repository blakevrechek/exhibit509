## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Southwestern Law School?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 157 (25th–75th percentile 154–160) and a median uGPA of 3.51 (3.23–3.72).

**What is Southwestern Law School's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 64.9% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 77.8% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Southwestern Law School cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $63,160 per year, and the median grant is $29,166; 28.2% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Southwestern Law School?**

83.4% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
