## Common questions

**What LSAT and GPA do I need for Washburn University?**

The most recent entering class had a median LSAT of 155 (25th–75th percentile 151–159) and a median uGPA of 3.68 (3.37–3.88).

**What is Washburn University's bar passage rate?**

The first-time bar passage rate was 71.1% for the most recent graduating cohort, versus a 80.0% state average on the same exam.

**How much does Washburn University cost?**

Sticker resident tuition is $27,028 per year, and the median grant is $15,000; 20.5% of students receive no grant.

**What are employment outcomes at Washburn University?**

92.9% of graduates were in full-time, long-term, JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation.
