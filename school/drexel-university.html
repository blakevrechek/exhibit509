Drexel University School of Law

Pennsylvania · Private · School ID: drexel-university

Drexel University School of Law is a private law school in Pennsylvania. In the most recent ABA Standard 509 cycle it reported a 85.0% first-time bar passage rate (state average 79.5%). 89.5% of graduates landed full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs, and 15.8% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. Resident tuition is $57,000 per year; a median grant of $28,667 brings median net tuition to about $28,333. The median LSAT is 160 with a 29.6% acceptance rate.

Frequently asked questions

What is the first-time bar passage rate at Drexel University School of Law?

Drexel University School of Law reported a 85.0% first-time bar passage rate in the most recent ABA Standard 509 disclosure, versus a 79.5% state average.

How much does Drexel University School of Law cost?

Resident tuition at Drexel University School of Law is $57,000 per year (about $171,000 over three years). The median grant is $28,667, bringing median net tuition to roughly $28,333 per year.

What LSAT and GPA do you need for Drexel University School of Law?

The median (50th-percentile) LSAT at Drexel University School of Law is 160 and the median GPA is 3.76; the acceptance rate is 29.6%.

What are the job outcomes at Drexel University School of Law?

89.5% of Drexel University School of Law graduates held full-time, long-term JD-required or JD-advantage jobs about ten months after graduation. 15.8% joined large firms of 251+ attorneys. 5.3% were still seeking employment.
